### Chimichurri Steak Sandwich

#### Ingredients

Serves: 2

+ Mint - 1/4 oz
+ Basil - 2 oz
+ Cilantro - 1/4 oz
+ Celery - 1 stick
+ White Onion - 1
+ Jalapeno - 1
+ Garlic Cloves - 2 
+ Kale - 2 oz
+ Lemon - 2
+ Olive Oil - 2 cups
+ Skirt Steak - 12 oz
+ Butter - 2 tbsp
+ Sub Buns - 2

#### Directions

Chop mint, basil, cilantro, celery, onion, jalapeño, kale, and garlic. Add to a bowl. Zest and juice the lemons into the bowl and season. Add enough olive oil to cover.

Pat dry steaks, season, and rub with olive oil. Cook steak on high in a cast iron pan. 

Toast buns.

Assemble.
