### Chocolate Chip Cookies

#### Ingredients

Serves: 18

+ Bread Flour - 1 cup
+ All-Purpose Flour - ¾ cup
+ Kosher Salt - 2 tsp
+ Baking Soda - 1 tsp
+ Unsalted Butter - 1 cup (2 sticks)
+ Dark Brown Sugar - 1 cup
+ White Sugar - ½ cup
+ Vanilla Extract - 2 tsp
+ Espresso Powder - 1 tsp
+ Egg - 1
+ Egg Yolk - 1
+ Semi-Sweet Chocolate Chips - ½ cup
+ Dark Chocolate (chopped) - 5 oz

#### Directions
Brown butter, pour into measuring cup (should be 1 cup, if not add water). Set aside to cool.

In one bowl sift together flours, salt, and baking soda.

In a separate bowl cream together (until fluffy) sugars, vanilla extract, espresso, and cooled brown butter. Now add egg & egg yolk to incorporate.

Add dry bowl to wet, mixing in ⅓ at a time.

Fold in chips and chopped chocolate.

(Ideally refrigerate overnight after scooping)

Bake at 350° Fahrenheit for 12-14 minutes.
