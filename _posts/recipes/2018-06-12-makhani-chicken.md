### Makhani Chicken

#### Ingredients

Serves: 6-8

+ Olive Oil - 6 tbsp
+ Garlic Cloves - 5
+ Birds Eye Chiles - 2
+ Long Red Chiles - 2
+ Yellow Onion (roughly chopped) - 2 medium
+ Ginger (peeled) - 3 thumbs worth
+ Ghee - ½ cup
+ Tomato Paste - 3 tbsp
+ Turmeric - 2 tbsp
+ Chili Powder - 2 tbsp
+ Garam Masala - 2 tbsp
+ Ground Coriander - 2 tbsp
+ Ground Cumin - 2 tbsp
+ Tomato Puree - 3 cups
+ Fenugreek Leaves (dried) - 1 tbsp
+ Chicken Breasts (boneless & skinless cut into 1 inch chunks) - 2 lbs
+ Heavy Cream - 2 cups
+ Unsalted Butter - 6 tbsp
+ Cilantro

#### Directions

Place the olive oil, garlic, chilies, onion, and ginger in a blender and purée until smooth.

Heat ghee in a large dutch oven over medium-high. Add the onion purée and cook until the mixture darkens slightly and softens, about 15 minutes.

Add the tomato paste and spices, cook for 5 minutes, or until dark.

Add in 1½ cups water. Using a wooden spoon, scrape up any fond.

Stir in the tomato puree and fenugreek leaves and increase the heat to high. Bring to a boil, then reduce the heat to maintain a simmer. Cover and cook, stirring occasionally, until thick, about 1 hour. Add the chicken and cook until the chicken is cooked through, about 15 minutes more.

Add the cream and butter and stir to combine. Season with salt and serve.

Garnish with cilantro and serve with rice.
