### Chocolate Mousse

#### Ingredients

Serves: 4

+ Graham Crackers - 2
+ Egg Yolk - 4
+ White Sugar - 6 tbsp
+ Semi-Sweet Chocolate - 5 oz
+ Heavy Cream - 1 cup
+ Vanilla Extract - 1 tsp

#### Directions

Make a double boiler, add yolks and 2 tbsps of sugar, whisk until smooth (should double in size). Add chocolate and whisk till melted, then add 2 tbsps of cream. Salt to taste and remove from heat.

In a separate bowl add remaining cream, sugar and vanilla extract. Mix on high until stiff peaks. Remove 1/2 cup of whipped cream for garnish.

In 2 batches fold in chocolate mixture to whipped cream (gently). Pour into serving cups and refrigerate for 3 hours.

Garnish with graham crackers and whipped cream.
